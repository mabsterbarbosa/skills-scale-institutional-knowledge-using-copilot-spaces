## Project management processes (brief summary)

OctoAcme follows a lightweight, repeatable program management approach:

- **Initiation**: capture objectives, scope, stakeholders, and success criteria.
- **Planning**: define deliverables, timelines, resource assignments, and risk mitigation plans.
- **Execution & Tracking**: implement work in short cycles with regular check-ins, status reporting, and transparent tracking of progress.
- **Risks & Communication**: identify and escalate risks early, maintain stakeholder communication plans, and record decisions.
- **Release & Deployment**: use checklists and gating criteria for releases to ensure quality and repeatable deployments.
- **Retrospective & Continuous Improvement**: run retrospectives to capture lessons learned and drive incremental improvements.

Refer to the linked documents for details, templates, and role responsibilities.
